KIBUYE TO GISENYI LONG TRIP

Embarking on a boat trip from Kibuye to Gisenyi (Rubavu) along Lake Kivu provides an unforgettable experience of Rwanda’s natural beauty.

This scenic journey, framed by lush hills and tranquil waters, offers a perfect opportunity for relaxation, cultural immersion, and wildlife spotting. Whether you're a nature enthusiast or a traveler seeking peaceful exploration, this boat trip is a delightful way to discover the charm of Lake Kivu.

SERVICES DESCRIPTION

The boat trip between Kibuye and Gisenyi is a leisurely voyage across Lake Kivu, offering passengers stunning panoramic views of the lake's expansive waters and surrounding hills. The journey allows for a slower pace, ideal for unwinding and taking in the beauty of the landscape. Along the way, travelers may observe small lakeside villages, traditional fishing boats, and local wildlife.

Both Kibuye and Gisenyi provide a range of accommodation options, allowing for a relaxing stay to explore each town's cultural attractions and culinary offerings. Local tour operators ensure a safe and environmentally conscious experience, making this boat trip a memorable and peaceful way to experience Rwanda’s lakeside treasures.

Additional Services

Rental Details

Your rental includes a light, inflatable kayak suitable for leisurely paddling. The kayak is easy to maneuver and perfect for exploring the calm waters of Lake Kivu.

Flexible Duration

You have the flexibility to choose your kayaking duration. If you plan to paddle beyond the initial hour, simply inform a staff member beforehand. Additional hours can be arranged, keeping in mind the hourly rental rate.

Safety and Comfort

Safety is paramount, with rental staff ensuring your kayak is in good condition and providing basic safety instructions. Life jackets are available to ensure a safe and comfortable experience on the water.

Cultural Discovery

Discover local communities, markets, and historical sites as you pedal through villages along the Congo Nile Trail or Kivu Belt Road. Engage with locals and learn about their traditions, enhancing your cultural experience.

Biking Adventure

Embark on a biking adventure along the picturesque paths of Lake Kivu, exploring the diverse landscapes and cultural attractions of the region. Whether you prefer solo exploration or cycling with a friend, biking allows you to discover hidden gems and scenic viewpoints.

Trail Exploration

Choose your route along the shores of Lake Kivu or venture into the nearby hills and villages. The biking trails offer a mix of terrains suitable for both leisurely rides and more adventurous exploration.
