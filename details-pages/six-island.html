SIX ISLAND TOURS

Lake Kivu is home to six beautiful islands, each with its own unique charm. Island hopping is a popular activity for visitors to the

area, offering the chance to explore the stunning scenery and experience the local culture. Whether you're looking for adventure

or relaxation, a day trip or an overnight stay, there's something for everyone on Lake Kivu's islands.

SERVICES DESCRIPTION

Our guided boat tour on Lake Kivu is the perfect way to explore the unique islands that dot the lake's surface. Launching from Kibuye, we'll take you on a journey to three different islands, each with its own character and history. From the historical Punishment Island to the light-hearted Farm Island, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Come walk among banana and coffee fields, hike, and jump from trees into the warm lake on Peace Island. Join us for an unforgettable adventure on Lake Kivu!
