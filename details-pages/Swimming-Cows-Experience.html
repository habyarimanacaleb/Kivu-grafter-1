SWIMMING COWS EXPERIENCE

The swimming cows of Mafundugu Island in Lake Kivu have become a captivating natural phenomenon. These remarkable animals exhibit a unique ability to swim between islands, a behavior that has intrigued locals and visitors alike. Their ability to navigate the lake's waters highlights both their strength and adaptability.

SERVICES DESCRIPTION

Every day, the cows of Mafundugu Island traverse the waters of Lake Kivu, moving from one island to another in search of better grazing areas. This impressive display of swimming skills is not only a testament to the cows' resilience but also showcases their deep connection to the environment.
