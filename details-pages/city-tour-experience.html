CITY TOUR EXPERIENCE

Embark on a biking and hiking adventure along the Kivu Belt Road, an immersive journey through Rwanda's stunning West Province. This tour blends outdoor adventure with cultural exploration, providing a unique opportunity to experience the region's natural beauty, historical significance, and vibrant local traditions. Along the way, you'll visit key landmarks, interact with local communities, and gain insights into Rwanda's past and present.

SERVICES DESCRIPTION

The journey begins with awe-inspiring views of Lake Kivu, a jewel of East Africa known for its rich biodiversity and striking landscapes. As the tour progresses, you'll delve into the poignant history of the Rwandan Genocide, visiting memorial sites that tell the story of resilience and healing. Vibrant local markets and traditional handicrafts offer a glimpse into Rwanda's cultural heritage, while visits to schools and churches highlight the role of community institutions in shaping the nation.

Throughout the adventure, you'll explore the diverse flora of the region, learning about native plants and their uses in local traditions. The tour encourages cultural exchange, fostering connections between visitors and locals, and emphasizes sustainable tourism practices to protect the environment.
